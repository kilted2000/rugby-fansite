Why Rugby is amazing.

It combines the best of Football and Soccer!

As action packed as soccer, but with the hits and physical battles of football,rugby has everything. It even comes with it's own dance,the Haka.

Rugby has a rich history going back nearly two hundred years.

With legendary beginnings in the 1820s, the oldest surviving rugby club dates back to 1843.

It is a truly international game.

It is played all over the world, from France to Japan. Each country brings its own flavor to the sport.

Absolutely anyone can play!

Young or old. Fat or thin. Experienced or newbie.Even if you don't know your ruck from your maul. There is a position for you on the team.
